Frequently Asked Questions



Do the applications run offline or only run online?


All our applications run both offline and online using any server of your choice.
Although, if you intend to use any of the applications to manage your business across multiple locations, it must be hosted online.


How many users does your application support?


Unlike some other business solutions out there, we are proud to inform you that there is no cap to the number of supported users.
With our applications, you can create as many users as possible to ensure smooth operations of your business and each of the users will function independently thereby guaranteeing you optimal output.


I am not in your country, how can I pay for your applications?


When you are purchasing any of our applications, the various payment options are PayPal, Skrill and through e-currency (USDT, TRX or BTC).
You can pay here or when you are ready to pay, click on Puchase App menu link. You may also contact us through our e-mail: info@haxerptech.com or on WhatsApp for assistance.
After your payment, you must notify us immediately to enable us confirm your payment and send you/configure your application for you through TeamViewer.


When will I get the application after payment?


As soon as we confirm your payment, your application shall be set up on your system directly. We do this through TeamViewer. You can read about TeamViewer here.

If you are a programmer and have paid, we shall send you the ZIPPED file containing instructions on setting up your application on local or live server.


What are your after-purchase services?

After purchasing our application, you can expect to receive various types of after-purchase services. These services may vary depending on the specific application you purchased. Here are some common after-purchase services we render:

Installation and Setup: We offer assistance in installing and setting up the application on your server or hosting environment. We may provide documentation, tutorials, or even remote assistance to ensure a smooth installation process.

Technical Support: We offer some form of technical support to help customers with any issues or questions they may have. This is done through email, live chat, or even phone support. Our team helps troubleshoot problems, provide solutions, and offer guidance on using the application effectively.

Bug Fixes and Updates: As with any software, our applications may have occasional bugs or require updates to improve functionality, security, or compatibility. So, we offer bug fixes and regular updates to ensure the application runs smoothly and remains up to date without extra charges for the first 2 years of use.

Training and Documentation: Depending on the application you are purchasing, we may offer training sessions, video tutorials, or comprehensive documentation to help users understand and utilize the application's features effectively. This can be particularly useful for new users or those who want to explore advanced functionality.

Customization and Integration: In some cases, we offer customization services to tailor the application to your specific needs. This could include branding, design modifications, or even adding new features. Additionally, we may provide guidance on integrating the application with other systems or platforms you use. Although this doesn't come for free.

Data Backup and Recovery: In case you want us to, we may offer backup and recovery services to ensure the safety of your information. This may involve regular backups, automated processes, or manual assistance. This comes also at extra charges.

License Renewal and Upgrades: We typically handle license renewals for anyone subscribing to our subscription-based applications. We offer options for upgrading to higher-tier plans with additional features or scalability options.

Remember that the specific after-purchase services we provide may vary, so it's essential to contact our support team directly to understand the scope and availability of these services.
